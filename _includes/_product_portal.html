Summary

As an IT Project Lead (and sometimes developer) at Student Trip! I took part in an awesome experience of helping to create their Student Trip! Portal. In technical terms, you could say I was the Product Manager for this new project.

Working closely with the lead UI/UX in-house expert and other business stakeholders (for requirements, features and buy-in), we were able to bring together a number of drafts that would later become the first iteration and released version of the portal. All pen/pencil sketches were me - I have a thing about doing things by hand.
